What is our process?
Our process is pretty simple right now. It's mostly manual until we implement our database into putting in the info that is entered on the sign up page, but once that is entered we input that data ourselves into the table. That is why we ask for 24 hours for your data to be inputted.
Why is my name not on the brackets?
There are a few reasons why your name wouldn’t be on the brackets. Check to see if the tournament is open. Sometimes we forget to close the form once the tournament is over or the sign up date has passed. Another reason is the 24 hour period. We have to manually check the entries until we implement another way of doing it.
The prize pool and what is in it.
It’s whatever we can come up with and it differs from tournament to tournament. We do have a donate button so if you like to donate to us to make the prize pool larger so others or you can win more then go right ahead. Check on the index page under our quick navigator in the top right and click on donate.
